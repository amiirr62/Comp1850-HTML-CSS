Final Project Proposal – Warehouse Inventory Management

Project Theme

The theme of this project is a Warehouse Inventory Management System designed to streamline the tracking, receiving, and shipping of flooring materials. The project simulates real-world inventory operations in a business setting.

Primary Objective

The website's primary objective is to provide a centralized platform for managing inventory operations such as:

  • Tracking available stock and sales reps
  • Receiving new materials
  • Shipping products to customers
  • Generating inventory and rep-based reports

Secondary Objectives

  • Improve internal communication between warehouse staff
  • Enable quick print/export of reports for physical recordkeeping
  • Demonstrate semantic, responsive web design best practices

Target Audience

The website is aimed at warehouse managers, internal staff, and company administrators. Specific audience characteristics:

  • Age: 25–60 years old
  • Location: Canada (or any English-speaking region)
  • Technical Skill: Moderate computer literacy
  • Language: English
  • Access: Desktop, tablet, or mobile devices in the warehouse

Information Architecture

The site uses simple and consistent navigation to access the following pages:

  • Home: Overview dashboard (index.html)
  • Receiving: Form for logging new materials (receiving.html)
  • Shipping: Form for tracking outgoing products (shipping.html)
  • Inventory: Filterable stock display (inventory.html)
  • Reports: Sales rep reports, with print functionality (report.html)
  • Sales Reps: View assigned jobs and materials by rep (reps.html)

Optional courtesy navigation may include print buttons and filter dropdowns within pages.
